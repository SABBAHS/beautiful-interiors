Bathroom & Kitchen


Beautiful interiors offers beautiful kitchen and bathroom interior designing services with budgets starting from £30,000. We would love to invite you to explore our solutions, and browse gallery of many design styles. Our designs range from modern cutting edge, contemporary, timeless classic and traditional to completely bespoke and unique to you and your style. We offer our services in procurement of all kitchen and bathroom selections including appliances, accessories and tiling. Custom made storage units, organisation and hidden cabinets for appliances you may want to cover for a consistent look. During our consultation we will discuss functionalality of our designs and any additional features such as lighting design, bathroom underfloor heating, colour schemes, and sample boards.

Architectural and Concept Interior Design


Adopting a bespoke approach to create tailored designs through lifestyle research for our clients. We offer comprehensive Cad drawings and concepts on a variety of rooms such as living, and bedrooms, as well as niche areas such as the playroom, walk-in-dressing room, and home office. Our clients can rest assured that we will organise the project management, on-site visits and measuring for each project. This includes the procurement of all interior selections including: decor, lighting, finish, colour, accessories, and fabrics. Every room no matter the size can be transformed, even the smallest room can be redesigned into anything using space-saving design techniques and furnishing.

Art Consultation & Cultural Fusion


Our art consultation service will better understand our clients tastes and takes you through a discovery of great artists and muralists. Whatever the home style contemporary, or traditional our team will be able to assist in selecting art pieces that bring out the character of the interior, and we arrange organised shipping, transportation and collection of every art piece. As well as selection, we provide an authentication service, conservation and restoration to breathe life back into art pieces. Cultural infusion in interior design is a great way to showcase our clients travels, inspiration or heritage in their homes. We carry out our research with deep cultural respect and understanding before presenting any design ideas. Our designers are experiences in sourcing out beautiful traditional fabrics, materials and art crafts to deliver nostalgic and cultured design.
